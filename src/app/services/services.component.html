About me

I am an Architect by academic qualification and a User Interface Designer. My area of focus has always been functionality, with an extended interest in aesthetics (the look and feel of the design). I care about functional and yet easy to use designs, I think about the product funnel and the user's journey through it. I also care about product research, data driven design, metrics, A/B testing, iteration, typography, mockups and quick turn around time.
